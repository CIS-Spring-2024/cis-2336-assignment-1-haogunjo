    • About Haminah Ogunjobi - Owner of Meenah's Food House

    Haminah Ogunjobi is the proud owner of Meenah's Food House, a culinary gem situated in the heart of the city. With more than two decades of experience in the culinary industry, Haminah is dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences for her customers.

    Haminah's journey in the food industry began during her formative years when she actively contributed to her family's restaurant. Through unwavering dedication and hard work, she refined her culinary skills, eventually embarking on her entrepreneurial journey. In 2010, Haminah introduced Meenah's Food House, quickly gaining acclaim for its delectable cuisine and inviting ambiance.

    Beyond managing the restaurant, Haminah actively participates in community initiatives and charitable events. She believes in giving back to the community that has steadfastly supported her business over the years.

    During her leisure time away from the bustling restaurant, Haminah cherishes moments spent with her family and stays abreast of emerging culinary trends. She consistently embraces the opportunity to experiment with fresh ingredients and innovative cooking techniques, ensuring a delightful experience for her cherished customers.
